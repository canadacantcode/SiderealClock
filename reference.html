References & Sources

These are the main sources we used to build our Mars and Earth sidereal and solar time clocks, and to learn about Karl Jansky and the history of radio astronomy.

Reference List

  • Sidereal Time — Wikipedia — used to understand the basics of sidereal time in general.
  • NASA Mars24 Algorithm Documentation — used for calculating Mars solar time and approximate Mars sidereal time.
  • MarsTI — Mars Time FAQ — used for inspiration and variable choices related to Mars timekeeping.
  • Cosmic Search, Vol. 1, No. 4 — “My Brother Karl Jansky” — used for historical background on Karl Jansky’s life and early radio astronomy.
