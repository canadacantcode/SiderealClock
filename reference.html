Reference & Sources

This page lists the main references we used to build our Mars and Earth sidereal and solar time clocks, along with background on Karl Jansky and radio astronomy.

References

  • Sidereal Time — Wikipedia — used to understand the fundamentals of sidereal time.
  • NASA Mars24 Algorithm Documentation — used for calculating Mars solar and sidereal time.
  • MarsTI — Mars Time FAQ — used for inspiration and understanding variables related to Mars timekeeping.
  • *Cosmic Search*, Vol. 1, No. 4 — “My Brother Karl Jansky” — used for historical background on Karl Jansky’s life and work.
